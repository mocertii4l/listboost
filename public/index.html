FAQ

Questions UK Vinted sellers ask.

Do I need to connect Vinted?

No. ListBoost is independent. You copy and paste everything manually — we never log in for you.

What does “one listing” mean?

One listing is one full output package: title, description, keywords, fast/fair/max price, photo checklist and a buyer reply.

What if I don’t like the output?

Edit any section in place before copying, or hit Reset to put the AI version back. Your edits never go back to the model.

Can I switch plans?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade from Billing after signing in. Changes apply right away.

What happens if I hit my monthly limit?

You’ll be asked to upgrade your plan to keep generating. Usage resets on the next billing cycle.

Is ListBoost connected to Vinted?

No. ListBoost is independent and is not affiliated with Vinted. Manual copy/paste only.
