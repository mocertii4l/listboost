FAQ

Real answers before you use ListBoost.

Is ListBoost officially connected to Vinted or any marketplace?

No. ListBoost is independent and manual-copy only. It is not endorsed by Vinted, does not use marketplace logos or screenshots, and does not connect to marketplace accounts.

Do I need to give ListBoost my Vinted password?

No. ListBoost never needs your marketplace password. You stay in control by reviewing the output, copying it, and posting it yourself.

Will ListBoost post items for me?

No. It prepares the listing content for you, including the title, description, keywords, photo checklist, price guidance and buyer preview. You choose what to use and post manually.

What can I create for free?

You can start with 3 free listings. You can also use the example page before signing up to see the type of output ListBoost creates.

Can I use my own messy notes and photos?

Yes. ListBoost is built for rough seller notes and normal camera-roll photos. It helps turn them into clearer listing copy and reminds you when important shots are missing, such as labels, soles, flaws or close-ups.

Does ListBoost guarantee more sales?

No. No listing tool can guarantee sales. ListBoost helps make listings clearer, more searchable and easier for buyers to trust, but results still depend on the item, price, demand, photos, shipping and marketplace activity.

How does price guidance work?

ListBoost suggests a practical price range from the item details, condition and selling context you provide. It is guidance, not a valuation or guarantee, and you always choose the final price.

What happens to my listing history?

Signed-in users can keep generated listings in their history so they can copy, edit and reuse their work later. Avoid adding sensitive personal information to listing notes.

Can I use it beyond clothes?

It works best for clothes, shoes, bags, jewellery, accessories and bundles. Other second-hand items can still work when you provide enough useful detail.

Can I cancel or change plan?

Yes. Paid plans are monthly and can be managed from billing after you sign in. Checkout and subscription billing are handled securely through Stripe.
