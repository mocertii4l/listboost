Pricing FAQ

Questions about plans, limits and billing.

Which plan should I choose?

Seller is best value for regular sellers. Starter suits occasional listings, Elite suits sellers who list every day.

What is one listing?

One listing is one full output package: title, description, keywords, fast/fair/max price, photo checklist and buyer reply.

Can I switch plans?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade from Billing after signing in. Changes apply right away.

What happens if I hit my monthly limit?

You’ll be asked to upgrade your plan to keep generating. Your usage resets on the next billing cycle.

Is ListBoost connected to Vinted?

No. ListBoost is independent and manual-copy only.

Do I need a card to try ListBoost?

No. Create a free account and try 3 listings before choosing a monthly plan.

Need help choosing?

If you’re unsure, start on Starter. Switching up to Seller takes a couple of clicks from Billing — no card juggling.
